Who I Am

Architecting Operational Resiliency

I am an Operations Strategist specializing in restaurant systems, operational excellence, menu engineering, process optimization, and hospitality innovation. My professional journey began deep inside fluid restaurant operations, where I quickly discovered that world-class restaurants are not built only by great food—they are built by intelligent systems.

Starting from an fundamental operational role, I progressed systematically into enterprise restaurant management before founding Gustavo Food Engineering. Today, my ultimate long-term vision is to systematically bridge traditional hospitality with modern automation pipelines, artificial intelligence, and completely data-driven operational management architectures.
