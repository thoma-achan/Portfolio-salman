01About

Who
I Am

I am Salman Ul Farisi, an Operations Strategist specializing in restaurant systems, operational excellence, menu engineering, process optimization, and hospitality innovation.

My professional journey began inside restaurant operations, where I quickly discovered that successful restaurants are not built only by great food—they are built by intelligent systems.

Starting from an operational role, I progressed into restaurant management before founding Gustavo Food Engineering, where I dedicated my work to helping restaurants improve efficiency through structured operations, documentation, workflow engineering, and process improvement.

My long-term vision is to bridge hospitality with technology, automation, artificial intelligence, and data-driven operational management.
